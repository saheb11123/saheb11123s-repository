About Garricks Maintenance

Garricks Maintenance has been opertaing in the Kensington and nearby suburbs for the past 25years, providing the best handyman services to its esteemed clients. We firmly believe in the motto that no job is too small and no job is too big.

We provide services in the suburbs in and around kensington with quality and customer satisfaction. We provide full repair and fixing services for our customers with top qualty products. We are skilled at all work around the house be it interior or exterior, we also provide basic and extreme renovation and handyman services to our customers with top quality products and provide a quality guarantee.

Our History

Garricks Maintenance is owned and operated by Mr Garrick Tuiit who has been a native of London, and has been operating and running Garrick's Maintenance Services for the last 25years.

We have been providing top-notch services to people in London and its surrounding suburbs for 25years. We pride ourselves with our work and strive to complete it in time and with precision so that the client is satisfied and happy.
